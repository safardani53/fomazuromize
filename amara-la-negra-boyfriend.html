Who Is 'Love &amp; Hip Hop: Miami' Star Amara La Negra's Boyfriend? She Just Gave Birth to Twins

 Posted on August 22, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  769 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Who is Amara La Negra's boyfriend? The reality star recently announced the birth of her twin daughters on Instagram. Details inside.

On VH1’s Love & Hip Hop: Miami, navigating the dating world can come with its share of drama. From messy divorces to love triangles, finding and nurturing a relationship is no easy feat for the stars. And since Amara La Negra experienced the downside of dating Emjay, Shay’s brother, who was considered to be a gold-digger, the songstress decided to play it even more safe on the romantic front.

Throughout Season 4, Amara decided to give love another try. Emjay’s efforts to get back into her good graces failed, and Amara moved on to a new man, Allan Mueses. However, difficulties in their relationship came to a head, which caused the couple to take a step back from their relationship.

Now that the news of Amara giving birth to twins has made its rounds online, fans are wondering if Amara is still connected to Allan. Here's the 4-1-1.

Amara and Allan Mueses recently announced the arrival of their twins.

Oh baby! Amara and Allan has officially expanded their family by two! On April 6, 2022, both Amara and Allan took to Instagram to announce the arrival of their twin daughters.

Allan wrote his tribute post in Spanish that translates as: “Grateful to father God for the blessing of becoming the father of two beautiful twins. I will love and protect you with everything." He added that the baby "already has many uncles and aunts waiting to see and hold her."

Amara also shared the fabulous news with a photo of herself in a hospital bed along with the caption "3/23," which seemingly confirms the twins' birthday.

Keep in mind, Allan was not present in any of Amara's photos in the hospital, which has led fans to wonder about Amara and Allan's relationship status.

Who is Amara La Negra's boyfriend?

After Amara announced in November 2021 that she was pregnant with her twins, she revealed that she was single, telling People en Español at the time: "I will be a single mother. I know my babies will depend on me. A father is not the one who makes the child, but the one who raises it. In time, if God grants me the blessing of finding the right man, one who supports me, who accepts me with my children, then Amen, he will be well received."

But, according to RealityTitbit, Amara and Allan have an on-again, off-again relationship. So, there is a chance that they may reconcile in the near future if they haven't already, especially since Amara confirmed that Allan is the father of her twins.

Article continues below advertisement

The pair initially met when Amara first started her journey in the real estate lane. On Season 4 of LHHMIA (which aired in 2021), Amara shared that she and Allan were exploring a romantic relationship with each other.

Article continues below advertisement

According to Allan’s Instagram bio, he works as a real estate expert and developer and is the CEO of the companies Soluciones Allan and Dream Big Construction Company.

He also has two daughters named Layla and Alanna. Allan frequently dotes on the girls on Instagram.

Before Amara had her twins, she sadly had a miscarriage.

Amara's rainbow babies have been a longtime coming, especially if you consider the start of Season 4. In the super-trailer, Amara suspects that she may be pregnant. After she speaks with a doctor and lets him know that she hasn’t had a menstrual period in three weeks, reality hits her.

But sadly, in a now-deleted post, Amara shared that she suffered a miscarriage in July 2021.

Article continues below advertisement

"Back in July, I found out I was pregnant," Amara wrote. "I didn’t even know or suspected [sic] being pregnant because I took all measures to prevent it. However, two weeks after missing my period, I started bleeding, having really bad cramps!"

Amara went on to explain that she initially believed that the bleeding and cramps were caused by her pre-existing condition of fibroids, but it turns out that she was actually having a miscarriage.

We are elated that Amara's fertility struggles are officially behind her! Congratulations are definitely in order for Amara and Allan on their double bundles of joy. We wish them all the best on their parenthood journey.
