Kanye West throws jab at ex Kim Kardashian by hanging out with fashion icon who snubbed her just day

 Posted on September 30, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  451 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

FANS claimed Kanye West dissed his ex Kim Kardashian by hanging out with a fashion icon who snubbed her just days earlier.

During New York Fashion Week, Kanye, 45, was spotted with the iconic Anna Wintour, 72.

Anna shared their meeting at the YZY 3022 SHDZ Activation event in an Instagram Story.

In the snapshot, the rapper and the editor-in-chief of Vogue stood in front of reflective mirrored walls.

The two had on slick ray-bans over their eyes as they smiled at the camera. 

Fans became convinced that the rap star was trolling his former partner as Anna wore the sunglasses from the business mogul's line.

ANNA'S SNUB

On September 11th, Kim, 41, was with actress Sarah Jessica Parker, 57, at the event when she was snubbed by the fashion icon.

Anna was walking towards the two as they were standing in the front row of the Fendi fashion show.

The twosome waved at the artistic director of Condé Nast.

Anna seemed to have ignored the reality star and went straight over to the Sex and the City actress. 

The pair hugged while Kim stood by awkwardly, smiling and looking down.

A short clip of their interaction made the rounds on social media.

Viewers mentioned that they felt "embarrassed" for the snubbed TV personality as Anna appeared to have "more respect for SJP's sense of fashion."

'I'M NOT LOOKING'

The Hulu star filed for divorce from her ex-husband Kanye in February 2021 after being married for about seven years.

The former couple share four kids: North, nine; Saint, six; Chicago, four; and Psalm, three.

The Kardashians star also split from Saturday Night Live funnyman Pete, 28, back in August after about nine months of dating.

During her appearance on James Corden's show, the late-night host asked Kim on what kind of man she sees herself with next.
