EMILY Atack oozed star quality as she arrived at Vanity Fair's Baftas pre-party in a glamorous black dress tonight.

The comedian and TV star, 33, looked great in the stylish black frock with a white feathered chest.

Her blonde hair framed her face as she posed for photographs at JOIA in London.

The great and the good of the entertainment world turned out for the exclusive event.

AJ Odudu, 34, showed off her supermodel-worthy figure in a revealing purple top that she wore with black flared trousers.

Pussycat Doll Ashley Roberts, 41, showed off plenty of flesh in an eye-catching outfit that just about covered her boobs.

Frankie Bridge, 34, looked flawless in a blue dress comprising of three distinct sections each attached to the next by threads.

Rapper Bugzy Malone looked smart in a cream suit and roll-neck, while Ella Eyre brought a welcome pop of colour with her bright yellow suit.

Fresh from cheering on her pal Cheryl in the West End last night, Nicola Roberts dazzled in a shimmering burgundy number.

The five nominees for the 2023 BAFTA EE Rising Star Award are Sex Education's Emma Mackey and Aimee Lou Wood, Whitney Houston actress Naomi Ackie, Good Luck To You's Daryl McCormack, and The Woman King's Sheila Atim.

Naomi said of her nomination: "I've followed and been inspired by the EE BAFTA Film Awards for so many years, so to be recognised amongst this incredible group of artists is huge! 

"This nod from the EE Rising Star jury, and to be recognised for my work in the country that I've grown up in, is also a nod of encouragement, to continue to create stories that speak to people. What a beautiful experience to be a part of."

The 2023 Baftas take place on Sunday, February 19.
