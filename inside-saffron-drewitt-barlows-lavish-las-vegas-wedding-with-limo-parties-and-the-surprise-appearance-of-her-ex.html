Inside Saffron Drewitt-Barlows lavish Las Vegas wedding with limo parties and the surprise appear

 Posted on September 5, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  855 words  |  Larita Shotwell

THE daughter of Britain’s first gay dads, Saffron Drewitt-Barlow, has tied the knot in a lavish Las Vegas wedding.

Proud parent and millionaire Barrie Drewitt-Barlow shared snaps from the celebrations, saying: “My baby got married today in Vegas!! Congratulations.”

Barrie and Tony made history cradling twins Saffron and Aspen — born to a surrogate in California in 1999 — as they revealed themselves as the UK’s first gay dads and the first same-sex couple to be named on a birth certificate as parents.

Millionaire Barrie split from husband Tony in 2019, but they still share a home in Florida so they can co-parent their family.

Saffron tied the knot at the Little White Wedding Chapel in Vegas in a one-sleeve white bodycon Prada dress, and the wedding party rode in a limousine.

Barrie, who is worth an estimated £175million from investments, shared a beaming photo standing next to his daughter, and their extended family.

The proud dad told Fabulous: "Saffron pulled it off in just two days.

"She managed to charter a jet from Tampa, get all her brothers and sister and I on it and surprised us by telling us she was taking us to Las Vegas so she could marry her long term boyfriend, millionaire property tycoon, Conor Tucker, from Cheshire.

"It was fast and it was tacky as hell but I still cried like a baby and it was so special still."

Saffron, who is expecting a baby in July, and her new husband posed next to a pink convertible at the chapel, which labels itself as a tribute to Elvis.

And another snap shows her husband lovingly cradling Saffron’s bump inside the chapel.

Barrie also uploaded a photo of the happy couple puckering up for a kiss during the ceremony.

Barrie added: "Saffron and Conor are planning to have a massive Essex style wedding in the summer next year at the church she was christened in with her twin Aspen 23 years ago.

"We had a private chef cook for us at Tao in downtown Las Vegas and after we spent the night gambling with all the boys."

Barrie and ex-husband Tony, 57, looked at adoption with Essex County Council, but went on to make history in 1999, after fathering twins Saffron and Aspen with a surrogate mum in California.

Manchester-born Barrie is the biological father of one, and Tony is the dad of the other — so they are actually half-siblings born at the same time.

They became the first British same-sex couple to be legally recognised as parents.

They went on to have son Orlando, 18, followed by 11-year-old twins Jasper and Dallas. 

Barrie also has son Colin, who is now in his thirties, from a previous relationship.

Sadly, Barrie and Tony split after growing apart, but they still remain committed to one another.

Tony was diagnosed with throat cancer in 2006. They started sleeping in separate bedrooms and their relationship became platonic.

The pair agreed to amicably split, but have continued co-habiting in their 10-bedroom mansion and are supportive of each other's relationships.

Bisexual Scott previously dated Barrie's daughter Saffron for several months, while working as his assistant. 

Scott became his 'shoulder to cry on' when Tony was in hospital during treatment in 2018 and a romance sparked between the pair.

They now share daughter Valentina and son Romeo.

Having got his daughter's blessing, Barrie proposed to Scott on a motor yacht near the Croatian island Hvar in August 2020, while the pair were on holiday with Saffron.

“For the past few years Scott has been under a lot of criticism for being with me," Barrie says.

“People have assumed that he is with me because of the money and that he couldn’t in any way be with me for anything else.

“But we are together because we love each other and he has always wanted to be able to stand on his own two feet.

“This is his way of showing that he can do his own thing.”

Saffron is expecting a baby this year, and Barrie revealed they can’t wait to spoil her.

He said: “I have to admit that we all can't stop buying for Saffron's little girl. 

“We are all excited.

“She will be the most spoiled little girl in the world, she has a wardrobe full of Gucci, Burberry, Versace, Stella McCartney and Dior already.

“So many that she couldn’t possibly even wear them all in the time frame for the age groups bought.”

Previously Barrie opened up about how his family celebrate Easter with a £8k bash.

Meanwhile, for Christmas they had an extraordinary £4million blowout in 2022. 

It included splashing more than a million quid on his and fiance Scott's baby Romeo, who was treated to a £475,000 Rolls Royce Cullinan to take him to nursery.
