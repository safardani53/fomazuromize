How Rich Is Ashton Kutcher? Net Worth, Salary, Career

 Posted on May 6, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  567 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Ashton Kutcher, a popular American actor, has shed 12 pounds since beginning training for the TCS New York City Marathon in 2022. The actor, who was previously suffering a rare autoimmune ailment, admits that his wife Mila Kunis has been quite supportive.

The actor from The Butterfly Effect revealed:

“The greatest physical shift has been the migration of muscular mass from the upper to lower body. I’ve dropped roughly 12 pounds. My wife has been quite helpful.”

Kutcher will run in the forthcoming marathon to raise funds and awareness for Thorn. Thorn is a non-profit group that strives to develop technologies to protect children from sexual abuse and to remove child sexual abuse content from the internet.

Ashton Kutcher’s Net Worth: What is his primary source of income?

The actor has been in the spotlight when word broke that he has lost a significant amount of weight. As of April 2023, the actor’s net worth is predicted to be $220 million as an American actor, producer, TV presenter, and entrepreneur. Ashton began his modeling career in 1998 when he competed in the IMTA competition, where he posed in Paris and Milan and featured in advertisements for leading brands such as Calvin Klein. After achieving popularity as a model, he relocated to Los Angeles because he wanted to pursue acting as a profession.

With That 70s Show, in which he portrayed Michael Kelso, Ashton Kutcher became the buzz of the town. The program ran from 1998 through 2006, and Ashton earned a lot of money from it. Following the success of this program, he went on to star in a slew of films and television series. He has appeared in over 25 films and 12 TV episodes, and he has also produced over 18 films. All of this has added significantly to his net worth.

Ashton Kutcher has starred in films such as Guess Who, A Lot Like Love, What Happens in Vegas, and No Strings Attached, among others. The actor has appeared in the renowned comedy Two and a Half Men. Apart from all of his initiatives in the entertainment business, his new foray into technology has significantly increased his net worth. He is a co-founder of the venture capital company A-Grade Investments. Furthermore, the actor is an active investor, having invested in over 60 startups including Skype, Foursquare, Airbnb, Path, and Fab.com.

Apart from business interests, Ashton Kutcher is also interested in real estate. In 2012, he paid $8.45 million for a home in the Hollywood Hills. The celebrity didn’t stop there. He and his wife, Mila Kunis, then spent $10.2 million on a second home in Beverly Hills. Having said that, Ashton is also a vehicle enthusiast who has a fleet of his own. Ashton Kutcher’s net worth is increasing year by year, due to his large investments and entertainment initiatives, with a collection of top luxury automobiles such as Lexus LS Hybrid, Mercedes SLK, Impala SS, Fisker Karma, and Tesla Model S.

Ashton and his motivation for running the marathon

Actor Ashton Kutcher explained why he is taking part in the event. According to the actor, his focus has always been his children and family. Another reason for running the marathon is to generate money and publicity for Thorn, the technology company he co-founded with his ex-wife, Demi Moore, in 2012. The organization is presently commemorating its tenth anniversary, and the marathon was held to commemorate the occasion.
