Who is Ryan Seacrest dating? The US Sun

 Posted on February 6, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  385 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

SINCE rising to prominence, Ryan Seacrest has dated a number of women.

The American Idol host has been romantically paired with Teri Hatcher, Julianne Hough, and Aubrey Paige.

Who is Ryan Seacrest dating?

Since 2021, Ryan Seacrest has been dating New York-based model Aubrey Paige.

Aubrey is repped by Genetics Model Management and is also an aspiring screenwriter.

Ryan and his former Live! co-host Kelly Ripa spoke of his relationship with Aubrey on their morning talk show.

Ryan recalled Kelly meeting Aubrey: "Kelly said, 'We're all happy you're here and that you exist because we weren't sure there was somebody.'"

Kelly commented: "We were starting to be like, 'Maybe he doesn't have a special person and maybe we should check in on him more often?' I am so fond of her."

However, there have been rumors of a split since Ryan was not seen in her Christmas celebration videos. Fans raised further questions about subsequent video she posted during the holidays highlighting Ryan's absence.

Who has Ryan Seacrest previously dated?

Since his rise to fame, Ryan has been romantically tied to a number of women.

From 2003 to 2005, the television host was in a relationship with Shana Wall.

Ryan and actress Teri Hatcher had a short-lived romance in 2006.

From 2010 through 2013, the American Idol host dated Julianne Hough of Dancing with the Stars.

Ryan started dating chef and influencer Shayna Taylor in 2013.

The pair were on and off until they called it quits in June of 2020.

A Live! spokesperson said of the former couple: "They remain good friends, each other's biggest supporters, and will always cherish their time together as a couple."

While Ryan and Shayna were off, reports surfaced of his alleged romances with model Renee Hall and Miss Teen USA’s Hilary Cruz.

What is Ryan Seacrest's net worth?

During Ryan's career, he took on various titles across a number of platforms - one of which included co-hosting the daytime talk show, Live with Kelly and Ryan, from 2017 to his departure on April 14, 2023.

In his lengthy entertainment tenure, he is a multiple Emmy Award nominee, winning one in 2010.

In 2008, he founded his own production company: Ryan Seacrest Productions.

According to Celebrity Net Worth, the popular mogul's net worth is $450 million, as of December 2023.
