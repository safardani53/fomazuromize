CONSIDERING how we're still in the depths of winter, our bikini collection is pretty much the last thing on our minds right now.

But whenever we open Instagram, there seems to be yet another celeb soaking up the sun in some far-flung location.

And while we might not be able to copy their exact holiday, there is something we can recreate in our own lives: their collection of skimpy string bikinis.

That's right, everyone from Kim Kardashian and Kylie Jenner to Emily Ratajkowski and Francesca Farago have been spotted rocking this trend.

And let's just say, it's not exactly the most practical choice of swimwear.

That said, it's almost guaranteed to take your Instagram "like" count into double figures.
