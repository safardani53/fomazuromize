MIKE Thalassitis lived up to his lothario status on the Celebs Go Dating finale last night when he begged date Celine and singer Talia Storm for a steamy threesome whilst on holiday in Cape Verde.

The scene may prove to be uncomfortable viewing for Towie’s Megan McKenna, as The Sun exclusively revealed their secret romance very soon after.

The trio were seen drinking at the bar together when Mike announced: “So obviously there’s three of us.”

The girls react in shock but Mike insists to the camera: “These two are absolute rockets.

"Three is definitely not a crowd and trust me, there’s plenty of Mike to go around.”

As the girls walk away, he tells them his room number – hoping they may change their mind.

Later, he’s spotted smooching Celine on the dancefloor and insisting they’ll still speak when he’s back home.

Mike announced filming for Celebs Go Dating had wrapped on February 1, sharing a group shot of the cast – including Talia – on his Instagram.

But he first sparked romance rumours with Megan in December when they were spotted spending Boxing Day together.

And their romance further intensified when they jetted out to Amsterdam for a romantic Valentine’s getaway – a mere two weeks after his threesome suggestion and smooches with Celine.

Despite her past ladies man status, Mike has appeared eager to prove himself to Megan.

Earlier this month, he even insisted she was The One.

During an interview, he insisted he would ‘marry’ the former TOWIE star.
