Sometimes Nicole "Snooki" Polizzi and Deena Cortese party a little too hard.

In Thursday's episode of Jersey Shore, the two Guidettes got so inebriated that they flashed their "cucas" while dancing at a nightclub in Riccione, Italy. "The nipple flap is one thing at the club," Jenni "JWoww" Farley said. "But you never forget your f–king underwear."

As their embarrassed costars looked on, Polizzi and Cortese began a heavy makeout session that lasted for hours. "Nicole and Deena are digesting each other's tongues," Farley, 25, said. "I am so skeeved out, I want to throw up."

Added Sammi "Sweetheart" Giancola, 24: "They've been making out for three hours. I don't even make out this long with Ron."

The next morning, Polizzi, 23, and Cortese, 24, struggle to piece their night together. "Usually even if I'm blacked out, I know I did sex," Cortese shrugged.

Realizing she was out of control, Polizzi called up her boyfriend Jionni LaValle to come clean about her drunken hookup.
