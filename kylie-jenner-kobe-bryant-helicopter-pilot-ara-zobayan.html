 Posted on March 17, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  594 words  |  Aldo Pusey

KYLIE Jenner has paid tribute to basketball legend Kobe Bryant following his tragic death yesterday.

The mom-of-one took to Instagram earlier today to share a picture of Kobe and the other eight people who lost their lives in the helicopter crash.

"Rest in peace... and prayers to these families. I still can't believe this," she captioned the post.

"That was the helicopter I would fly on from time to time with that pilot, Ara. He was such a nice man. Hold your loved ones close."

Kylie rented the helicopter as recent as November last year for her niece Dream's third birthday.

The post comes after the 22-year-old shared a picture of Kobe, 41, and his 13-year-old daughter Gianna, who also died in the accident.

Kylie captioned it: "At loss for words right now. Praying for this beautiful family."

Tributes have been pouring in for the NBA legend since news broke of the air crash.

Kobe's private Sikorsky S-76 crashed into a hillside in Calabasas, California, about a mile away from the home of Kylie's older sister, Kourtney Kardashian.

Kobe and Gianna lost their lives alongside seven other people when the helicopter they were in plummeted nearly 500 feet in just 15 seconds before smashing into the Los Angeles hillside.

Kourtney, 40, paid her condolences by sharing a couple of photos of Kobe with his children on her Instagram story.

She captioned it: "My heart is with Vanessa and her babies," referring to Kobe's long-term wife.
