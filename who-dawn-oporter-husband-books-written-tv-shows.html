Who is Dawn O'Porter, who's her husband, what books has she written and what TV shows has she presen

 Posted on March 14, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  441 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

SHE’S the TV host who is famous for shows like Super Slim Me and Extreme Wife – and for her distinctive bob hair cut with a blunt fringe.

But who is Dawn O’Porter, how did she become famous and what shows has she presented?

Who is Dawn O'Porter?

Dawn O’Porter is a Scottish writer and TV presenter who shot to fame after attempting to slim down to a size zero for BBC documentary Super Slim Me.

She was born in 1979 and was raised in Guernsey with her aunt and uncle following the tragic death of her mum, Carol Rix, who died of breast cancer when Dawn was six.

She has two sons with her husband; Art, who was born in January 2015, and Valentine, who was born in July 2017.

Dawn has her own TV production company called Hot Patootie TV and also is a published author.

Who is Dawn O'Porter’s husband?

Dawn is married to Irish actor Chris O’Dowd, who is famous for starring as Roy Trenneman in the IT Crowd and for films such as Bridesmaids and This is 40.

He married Dawn in 2012 and she later changed her surname to O’Porter to reflect this.

Chris is an actor from Boyle, County Roscommon, who grew up as the youngest of five kids.

He played Gaelic football at county level up to the under 21 age group, and studied politics and sociology at University of Dublin.

But he didn't finish with a degree, after deciding he had no interest in sociology.

He then upped sticks and moved to Britain, to study at the London Academy of Music and Dramatic Art.

What books has Dawn O'Porter written?

In 2006, Dawn released her first book called Diaries of an Internet Lover.

And later in May 2013, Dawn published her first fictional novel, Paper Aeroplanes, which was a tale about female friendships inspired by her Guernsey upbringing.

She has also written for a number of newspaper and magazines in the UK, including The Sunday Times and Glamour.

What TV shows has Dawn O'Porter presented?

Dawn has been at the helm of a host of TV shows, including her own BBC Three series of four documentaries called Dawn…

These were released in 2008 and explored themes such as attitudes towards nudity, lesbianism, dating and pregnancy.

Later that year she produced a series called Extreme Wife on Channel 4, and in 2009 ghosted a documentary about breast cancer called The Booby Trap for Sky 1.

Recently she hosted and co-produced a Channel 4 series called This Old Thing about her love of vintage clothes.
