The pair have been sleeping together over the past few months in secret.

Ange, who is 25 years older than the junior doctor, could definitely be putting her career at risk considering the huge age gap.

"Is it true?"

Ange simply replied: "Yes."

The camera then panned to Josh and Henrik asked him: "Is the relationship of a sexual nature?"

The junior doctor replied: "It is.."

Henrik asked: "Has it been taking place on hospital premise?"
