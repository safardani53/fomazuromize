Teresa Giudice and Luis Ruelas Wedding Photos Gallery

 Posted on July 4, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  354 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Happy anniversary, Teresa Giudice and Luis Ruelas!

The lovebirds are celebrating one year of marriage on Aug. 6, 2023, and to mark the milestone, we're looking back on their Housewives-filled wedding in East Brunswick, New Jersey, last summer.

From the big hair to the big guest list, revisit some of the wedding day's memorable moments.

Jersey Girl

The bride's dress was a custom Mark Zunino Atelier blush mermaid gown that featured 300-plus yards of hand-draped English netting. The train was more than 100 in. long, covered in crystal and pearl details with long white beaded evening gloves to match. Her veil was embroidered with three hearts and the words 'sempre insieme,' which mean 'always together' in Italian.

Dorinda Medley and the Newlyweds

Fellow Real Housewives stars like New York City's Dorinda Medley were on hand to celebrate.

"A beautiful wedding filled with love, friends, laughter and happiness. Thank you for letting me be a part of this magical night," Medley wrote on Instagram.

The Giudice Girls

Also making for beautiful bridesmaids, Teresa's daughters Gia, 22, Gabriella, 18, Milania, 17, and Audriana, 13, whom she shares with her ex-husband Joe.

Loni Love and Cynthia Bailey

Bailey was feeling the Love as she puckered up for a selfie with The Real host.

Kenya Moore and Chanel Ayan

Ayan made fast friends with Moore. "Kenyan Somali girl meets the queen KENYA," Ayan wrote on Instagram.

Ashley Darby and Chanel Ayan

Real Housewives of Potomac's Ashley Darby also hung out with Ayan at the reception. "This BEAUTY!!" she wrote with a photo of them on her Instagram Story.

Ashley Darby and Phaedra Parks

Darby posed for a photo with fellow mother Parks as well. "Baddie boy moms," she wrote.

Alexia and Todd Nepola

The Real Housewives of Miami pair brought the drama.

Housewives Assemble!

Hitting the dance floor, RHONJ newcomer Jennifer Fesler posed for the ultimate group photo with Darby, Josephs, Ayan, Bailey, Moore, Medley, Parks and Goldschneider.
