What Is Jennette McCurdy's Relationship History? Details

 Posted on June 7, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  524 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

'iCarly' star Jennette McCurdy's past relationships include a fling with an NBA player and a few other surprises. Here's her full relationship history.

She may be retired from acting, but Jennette McCurdy still has plenty of cultural cachets. The former iCarly star has just released her new memoir, I'm Glad My Mom Died, in which she details her childhood stardom, feuding with Ariana Grande, and her tumultuous relationship with her mother, Debra.

In light of that memoir, some are curious to know about Jennett's relationship history — or at least the relationships that have been public. Here's what we know.

Graham Patrick Martin

Although the rumors have never been confirmed, there was speculation that Jennette dated actor Graham Patrick Martin when they were in their teens. Graham even made an appearance on iCarly in 2009. Today, he's in a relationship with actress and influencer Dominique Druckman.

Max Ehrich

Long before his short-lived engagement to Demi Lovato, some fans were convinced that Max Ehrich dated Jennette following his 2010 appearance on iCarly. Eventually, Max even had to come out and reveal that, while they definitely liked each other platonically, he and Jennette had never been anything more than friends.

Article continues below advertisement

Andre Drummond

NBA star Andre Drummond initially connected with Jennette over social media, but they eventually brought that bond into reality when the two started dating in 2013. Some were shocked to discover this recently, partly because it seems like such an unlikely pairing. Ultimately, though, the relationship between Jennette and Andre seems to have been pretty short-lived, ending the following year.

Article continues below advertisement

Paul Glaser

During a 2015 interview with Vulture, Jennette confirmed that she had previously dated Paul Glaser, who worked as a script manager on iCarly. Jennette even shared some photos from this relationship on social media. While it seems the two had some nice times together, Jennette broke up with Paul after learning that her mother's cancer had returned, saying that she needed to deal with the loss of her mother alone.

Article continues below advertisement

Jesse Carere

In that same Vulture interview, Jennette revealed that she had been dating her Between co-star Jesse Carere for more than a year. It's unclear exactly how or why the relationship ended, but fans took note when she removed many of their pictures from social media in 2016.

When speaking to Vulture, Jennette was open about her relationship and her thoughts about love. “I feel like I’m learning to love in all the right ways and to accept love for who I am versus an impressive version of myself,” Jennette said at the time. “I think from an early age I learned to be whatever person people needed me to be.”

However, since 2016, Jennette has been pretty quiet about who she may or may not be dating. While it's possible and perhaps likely that she's been in other relationships in recent years, the public hasn't been made aware of those. Jennette has decided to take a bit of a step back from the limelight and value her privacy more.
