Jessica Simpson fans slam her ex Nick Lachey for 'toxic' and 'mean' behavior in resurfaced Newlyweds

 Posted on September 7, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  845 words  |  Larita Shotwell

JESSICA Simpson's fans defended the singer after watching her ex Nick Lachey's "mean" behavior towards her in a resurfaced Newlyweds clip.

The former couple were married for four years and documented much of their relationship on the MTV reality show.

Viewers reminisced over the two decades old series on TikTok, but some found the throwback difficult to watch, branding her then-husband as "toxic."

In the video, the pair were preparing to perform together for the first time in two years at the USO Show in Hollywood.

Upon arriving to their hotel along with Jessica's parents, Tina and Joe, the two got into a spat after the Irresistible singer admitted she hadn't eaten.

Her mother suggested that her lack of food had affected her mood, to which the songstress claimed she had no time.

Instead of comforting his wife, Nick made some nasty remarks towards Jessica prompting her to clap back at her hubby.

"You haven't seemed half interested in half the things I've said today," she said in response to the 92 Degrees singer's comments.

He fired back: "Because everything coming out of your mouth is you crying about something, I'm not interested."

The arguments escalated when Jessica grew jealous over Nick calling another woman "cute" and asking him how he viewed her.

The Ohio native again replied with a nasty remark saying: "The way you're behaving today is hardly womanly."

The fight carried on as the group began talking about the show, in which Jessica confessed she wasn't happy with the preparation on her end.

Her parents attempted to ease her worries about the show not being "cool" by mentioning that the male dancer she has on stage will enhance the performance.

This prompted Jessica to comment about her husband's performers saying: "Well I can make a joke about how my husband has hot girls that I get to have some kind of hot soldier up there on stage with me."

The following scene showed Jessica getting ready to head to the venue and noticing that Nick had already left without her.

She voiced her frustrations saying that she was upset with her husband for not telling her he was leaving.

"He didn't even say I'm leaving, walk down with me. No, he's got two pussycats on his arm," she ranted still not over their previous fight.

They eventually kissed and made up prior to heading on stage, but fans were still uncomfortable with how Nick treated his wife.

FAN BACKLASH

One user commented: "Jessica needed a friend during that time. Everyone was so mean to her 😩"

While a second person thought that Nick's harsh words were out of line, writing: "My husband would never talk to me like that in front of my family."

Another agreed, adding: "I didn't realize how toxic he was to her until now as an adult."

A fourth person chimed in: "I don’t like how mean and condescending he always to her. She WAS young, always demeaning her for not being a woman"

And a slew of others echoed the statement, with one saying: He was so awful to her.

"Omgosh they where so toxic," wrote another.

Nick and Jessica were married from 2002 until 2006.

Their lives were filmed on the show from 2003 until it's final episode in 2005.

TELLING MEMOIR

In her memoir Open Book, Jessica spoke about how their marriage unraveled after the pair filmed their reality show.

In February 2020, she explained, "I don't believe that the show is what tore our marriage apart."

"Nick and I were very great at being together publicly and on-camera. We were best at our relationship when we were singing together. I felt like we were at home in that place," Jessica continued.

"But as far as doing the reality show, we just kind of had fun with it until the end—until we started having marital problems."

Following the release of her book, Nick appeared on SiriusXM's Andy Cohen Live in November 2021, where he revealed he has no plans on reading his ex-wife's book.

When Andy asked if he had read the book, Nick responded: "You’ll be shocked to hear that I have not. Nor will I ever read that book."

MOVED ON

He continued: "I obviously knew the book was coming out, but I lived the book. You know what I mean.

"I know what the truth is so I don’t need to read it, or read someone’s version of it in a story that I know, that I lived it. No, I never read it."

Both have since moved on to new love interests as Nick married actress Vanessa Minnillo in 2011 after five years of dating.

They went on to have three children together: Camden, nine, Brooklyn, seven, and Phoenix, five.
