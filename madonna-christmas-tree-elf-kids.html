Madonna, 63, dresses up as an elf as she decorates Xmas tree with kids at LA home

 Posted on June 19, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  142 words  |  Larita Shotwell

MADONNA is getting Into The Groove of Christmas with her latest outfit.

The Queen of Pop, 63, decided to express herself in an elf themed onesie.

Breaking with tradition, Madge decided to cover up as she decorated her Christmas tree with her family.

Joined by David Banda, 16, Mercy James, 15, and twins Estere and Stella, both nine, the superstar sported long blonde hair as she posed for a series of playful snaps.

Despite being the Material Girl, Madonna was keen to tell fans there is more to the festive season that presents.

Madonna captioned her post: “Let’s embrace all faiths and rituals as long as they make us happy and we are focused more on giving than receiving.”
