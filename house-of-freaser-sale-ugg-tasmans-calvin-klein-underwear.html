 Posted on August 25, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  565 words  |  Larita Shotwell

SHOPPERS are racing to House of Fraser after the department store slashed the price of its designer stock.

The in-demand Ugg Tasmans, Calvin Klein multi-packs, trendy trainers and Jack Wills lounge wear have all been heavily discounted.

The sale has gone viral on Facebook, with shopper Emma Louise Mathieson nabbing £219 worth of stuff for just £45.50.

That's a whopping £173.50 worth of savings.

Fabulous takes a look at the top picks from House of Fraser's stylish sale.

Ugg Mania 

Had your eye on some Ugg boots to keep your toes warm this winter? Enter, House of Fraser sale.

Bailey Button boots are now £129, Short Boots are now £129 and the Tall 2 boots have been slashed to £169 - making all of them roughly £50 cheaper than full retail price. 

The Aussie brand’s summer shoes are heavily discounted too for anyone getting their wardrobe ready for summer.

The Ugg Sport Yeah sandals are 50% cheaper at £30, as are the Puft Slide sandals at £55. 

Sandals from the Ashton range have also been reduced, and you can pick up a pair costing anywhere between £50 to £70… not bad for shoes which are normally triple figures, is it?

If the man in your life wants a pair of the iconic Tasman Slippers, House of Fraser has dropped the men’s dark grey versions to £80 from £100. 

Calvin Klein Undies 

They’re a staple in most knicker drawers but with prices creeping up, it’s hard to keep fully stocked. 

Luckily, House of Fraser has slashed the price of Calvin Klein underwear in its New Year sale. 

Among the bargains are thongs, full-brief knickers, multi-packs, lacy bras, sports bras and more. 

The five-packs of thongs and carousel briefs have been dropped to £47 from £65, while three-packs of bikini knickers are now £28, down from £40. 

The American designer’s signature cotton bralettes start at £7 in the House of Fraser sale and lacy bras will set you back around £15, rather than the usual £45. 

New Balance Kicks 

New Balance have been cult sneakers to have in your footwear arsenal for nearly three years now.

And their popularity isn’t set to die down, so the House of Fraser sale is the perfect place to nab a pair. 

The sporty shoes are being flogged for as cheap as £34. 

Among those discounted are the quirky MR530s to £68 from £95, comfy 237s to £72 from £90 and in-demand 480s also down to £72 from £90. 

If these prices are still out of your budget, Fabulous reported last week that M&S is flogging dupes of New Balance 530s.

Shoppers said that the trendy trainers are “easily mistaken” for the branded versions.

Jack Wills Comfies

Famed for its hoodies and sweatshirts, the Jack Wills sale at House of Fraser is a dream for fans of the British brand.

There’s tracksuits and knitted sets galore, all of which are perfect for lazing around in the cold weather. 

And the best bit? They’re yet to sell out of most sizes, meaning you’ll definitely find a bargain.

If knitted loungewear is your vibe, you can pick up the grey marl Lounge Knitted set for just £21 - with the hoodie costing £10 and the joggers £11.
