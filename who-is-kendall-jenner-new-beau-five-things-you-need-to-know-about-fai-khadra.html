Kendall Jenner has always been coy about her romances whilst the filming of Keeping Up With The Kardashians.

We know she’s had some famous boyfriends in the past… Justin Bieber, Harry Styles, ASAP Rocky… any of those names ring a bell?

But she’s been even more private about her most recent romance.

Here are five things you need to know about Kendall’s potential new beau, Fai Khadra!

Fai Khadra.

Who is Fai?

Fai is 27-years-old.

He was born in Saudi Arabia to a wealthy Palestinian family, but he and his sisters were raised in Dubai.

Fai’s mother, Rula, owns a boutique in Saudi Arabia called The Art of Living and his father is an art collector.

It certainly seems that Fai’s parents are a creative pair, and this has definitely rubbed off on their children.

What does Fai do?

It seems that nowadays Fair just sticks to modelling here and there.

He posts lots of his modelling pictures to his Insta @yourboifai, where he has near 300k followers.

But he started off his career as a singer and in 2015 he released a single called ‘I Love It’.

Although his track didn’t hit mainstream success, it certainly shows off his talent.

He has yet to release any solo music since then.

Who are his sisters?

He is the older brother of the Insta famous modelling and DJing twins, Sama and Haya Khadra, more commonly known as SimiHaze.

Simi and Haze are both great pals of the Kardashian-Jenner’s, but also Gigi and Bella Hadid and Hailey Baldwin.

We presume that’s how Fai and Kendall got to know each other…

Who has he dated before?

Fai has had his fair share of A-lister girlfriends.

He dated Victoria’s Secret model Devon Windsor for a couple of years and the two were very serious.

Fai has even been spotted out with Jordyn Woods back in 2017 – another reason she is going to remain excommunicated from the Kardashian-Jenner family.

At one point back in September 2018 he was even linked to Kendall’s older sister Kourtney!

So is he dating Kendall?

Well, nothing is confirmed, but Fai’s presence has been felt all over Kendall’s Instagram.

Everyone has been suspecting something between the pair since their joint Austin Powers Halloween costume in 2018.

And as he made an appearance in episode 1 of Keeping Up With The Kardashians season 16.

Despite the fact he was dubbed ‘Kendall’s friend’ on the show, the two are clearly becoming more than just friends and often get spotted out on lunch dates.
