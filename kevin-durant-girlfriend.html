Who is Kevin Durant?

Kevin Durant is a basketball player who plays for the Phoenix Suns of the NBA. He was born on September 29, 1988, and is 35 years old as of 2023. 

Kevin’s mother, Wanda, has a movie made based on her life.

Durant won two NBA Championships with Golden State Warriors in 2017 and 2018. Later, he had to join the Nets as a free agent due to injuries.

His height is 2.08 m, and he weighs around 240 lb. The Seattle SuperSonics drafted him in 2007 as the second pick.

He has a great shooting range and a good ability to finish at the free-throw line.

This world-class athlete is one of the best scorers in the game. He started his professional career in 2007 with on-and-off injuries.

Besides his basketball career, he has also entered the movie industry. He made his acting debut in Thunderstruck, directed by John Whitesell.

Kevin produced Two Distant Strangers, which took Oscar home for the best live-action short film in 2021.

He was traded with Mikal Bridges and T.J. Warren to the Suns in exchange for Cameron Johnson, Jae Crowder, four unprotected first-round picks, and a 2028 first-round pick on February 9, 2023.

Durant was named the Eastern Conference starter for the 2023 NBA All-Star Game on January 26, 2023.

As of Week 20 in the NBA, fans are eyeing Durant’s return. He planned to return after a three-day break after an MCL injury.

However, his team Phonix Suns, plans to let him play on March 1 against the Charlotte Hornets.
