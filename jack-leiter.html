Jack Leiter – Baseball Career

Jack was drafted as No. 615 overall in the 20th round of the Major League Baseball (MLB) Draft by the New York Yankees. They projected him as the first-round pick.

They offered him $4-$4.5 million, as per the sources. But Jack chose Vanderbilt and the guidance of Coach Tim Corbin.
