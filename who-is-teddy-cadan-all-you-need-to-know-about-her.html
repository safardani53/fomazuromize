Her Parents

She was born to parents Lena Headey and Dan Cadan. Her parents were childhood friends who never imagined they’d end up marrying. Lena once stated, “I constantly told him, ‘Don’t ever ask me to marry you, it’s a catastrophe.'” But it feels great.” She was born two years before their engagement. After four years of dating, the couple married in 2018. The husband and wife are enjoying a happy marriage with their daughter Teddy.
