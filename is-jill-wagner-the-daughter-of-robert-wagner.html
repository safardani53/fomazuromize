Jill Wagner’s Rise to Stardom

A North Carolina native, Jill Wagner was born on January 13, 1979. The actress and her older brother were raised in their hometown by their grandmother and US Marine father after losing their mom when Jill was still a baby.

Jill completed her college education in 2001 and soon afterward, she decided to make a living as a model.

What Happened to Jill Wagner?

In March 2008, it was announced Wagner would be the co-host for the ABC summer reality series Wipeout which premiered June 24, 2008.

She was succeeded by Vanessa Minnillo for the series’ fifth season.
