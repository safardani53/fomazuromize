I swapped my morning Starbucks coffee for a $2 Aldi alternative - it comes in three different option

 Posted on January 1, 0001  |  2 minutes  |  282 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

ALDI is known as a grocery store with great deals and low prices, and opting for some of the chain's products could make a big difference in helping out your wallet.

Shoppers are saying that Aldi's in-house coffee brand is just as good as a morning cup of Starbucks - for only two dollars.

The Barissimo Iced Coffee is a ready-to-drink mixture available in French Vanilla, Mocha, and Coffee Latte flavors.

Delish describes the iced coffee as a brand "you'll be grateful for every morning."

"Its product mix includes iced coffee, whole beans, cups, and more."

Along with the already-prepared iced drink, Aldi has coffee cups, ground, and instant coffee through the same brand.
