Derek Henry Carr was an English footballer who played in the Football League for Birmingham City. Carr, a utility player, joined Birmingham City from Lockheed Leamington in December 1947, and made his debut in the First Division on 22 October 1949 in a goalless draw away at Everton. He played twice more in the 194950 season, and also worked in the offices at St Andrew’s, before moving into non-league football with Rugby Town, Evesham Town and Boldmere St. Michaels.

He played three seasons at Clements High School in Texas before spending his senior year at Bakersfield Christian High School in California.
