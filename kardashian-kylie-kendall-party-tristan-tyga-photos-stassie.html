KARDASHIAN fans are enraged after blacklisted family members including Tristan Thompson have attended Kylie Jenner’s BFF’s birthday party.

Kylie, her sister Kendall Jenner, bestie Stassie Karanikolaou, Khloe's ex Tristan Thompson and Kylie's ex Tyga were all in attendance at the wild event.

Several photos of celebrities entering and exiting the party made their way to social media and fans were shocked at the interesting guest list.

The Kardashians' sister Khloe, 37, was heartbroken when the news of her baby daddy Tristan, 31, cheating on the Kardashian with Maralee Nichols, 31, broke to the public media in December 2021.

Even though the couple had navigated a rocky relationship through the years, this was the final blow and Tristan was blacklisted by the Kardashian family.

Kylie dated Tyga and broke it off in 2017 before moving on with Travis Scott.

TRISTAN'S TRIBULATIONS

Tristan revealed that he was the father of Maralee's baby a month after the child was born.

He shared the news on social media and offered an apology to Khloe. 

Although the Canadian-born athlete confessed to being the child's father, he reportedly remains uninvolved in the boy's life. 

Maralee is suing him for child support.

Tristan has allegedly not met his lovechild, even while becoming locked in a legal battle against the boy's mother.

Maralee accused the Chicago Bulls player of being an absent father since he has reportedly refused to pay child support.

In March 2022, she demanded the basketball player pay about "$47K" a month and $1million in legal fees.

The case remains ongoing, although Khloe recently broke her silence on the scandal.

'HEARTBREAK' CLUB

On Thursday, Khloe got candid with her Twitter followers about cameras capturing the moment her family found out about Tristan's love child scandal.

Khloe confessed in a tweet: "Rewatching this part is uncomfortable but I love and appreciate my family more than I can express.

"Praise the Lord I have them for the rest of my life."

Khloe has largely been silent about the scandal since it broke late last year.

LOVE STORY

As for Kylie and Tyga, the pair met in November of 2011 at her sister Kendall's 16th birthday party when Kylie was 14 years old and Tyga was 21.

They went public on social media in 2014 as Kylie posted photos of them celebrating her 17th birthday together.

In September of 2016, Kylie showed off a massive ring on Instagram leaving her fans wondering if she and Tyga were engaged or not.

A year later they confirmed their split, just weeks before the paparazzi caught Kylie and her current boyfriend Travis holding hands at Coachella.
