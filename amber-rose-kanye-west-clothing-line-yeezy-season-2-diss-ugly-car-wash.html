“Amber feels bad for everyone who attended what she thinks is a disaster of a show,” an insider tells HollywoodLife.com EXCLUSIVELY. “She especially feels awful for the models that had to wear that nonsense. They looked sad and very uncomfortable, as if the fabrics were itching them. Amber saw most of the outfits and would absolutely not wear them.”

But Amber — whose book you can buy here — doesn’t want the clothes to go to waste, of course! Instead, she’s offering Yeezy another idea. “She thinks he should take those clothes and open up a car wash!” our source reveals. “That oversized attire and itchy-looking fabric look like they’d be perfect for drying off cars. And Amber wants 20 percent off the top for giving him the idea!”

Whoa, a little harsh, no!? The 31-year-old wasn’t the only one to diss the clothing line, either. Chris Brown, 26, made quite the statement on Instagram after the fashion show, by posting a video with Caitlyn Jenner superimposed into the slew of models, spewing out comments that totally trash the clothes. Eek, why, Chris?!

Regardless of Amber and Chris’ thoughts, though, Kanye definitely has a lot of support. Kylie Jenner, 18, modeled in his show, and Kim, Kourtney, Khloe and Kendall were all cheering from the crowd. Even Anna Wintour was sitting front row to show encouragement for the rapper-turned designer. Not a bad crew to have on his side, right?!
