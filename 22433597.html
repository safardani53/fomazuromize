Haaland and girlfriend Isabel wear matching silk pyjamas as Man City stars and Wags party at nightcl

 Posted on June 6, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  256 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

ERLING HAALAND and his girlfriend Isabel Johansen wore matching outfits at Manchester City’s title celebrations.

The squad and the wags met up for a night out after lifting the Premier League trophy following the 1-0 win over Chelsea.

The party began in the City dressing room after full-time with stars drinking beer and eating pizza.

And the celebrations continued in a Manchester nightclub, with record-breaker Haaland spotted in an unusual outfit.

The striker, 22, wore silky sky blue pyjamas bearing his initials and was matched by his partner Isabel, who looked a little more stylish in a silk dress of the same colour.

His father Alfe-Inge Haaland was also in attendance after watching the Norwegian collect his first Premier League medal earlier in the day.

Phil Foden, Kyle Walker and John Stones were all seen alongside their partners.

While Kevin De Bruyne’s wife Michele Lacroix stunned in a braless see-through top.

Riyad Mahrez was joined by his social media star wife Taylor Ward, who boasts three million followers on Instagram.

And Bernardo Silva met up with his team-mates alongside soon to be wife Ines Tomaz – the pair are set to tie the knot in Portugal in July.

City players and staff were also accompanied by members of Manchester band Blossoms, including lead singer Tom Ogden and drummer Joe Donovan.

Manuel Akanji, who is celebrating his first title win with City, arrived with his wife Melanie Windler.

Rodri and his partner Laura also showed up along with captain Ilkay Gundogan and his wife Sara Arfaoui.
