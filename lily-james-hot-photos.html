Happy birthday Lily James! Lily, born April 5, 1989, is an English actress, known for her role as Cinderella and Pamela Anderson. She began studying the craft as a teenager by attending Tring Park School for the Performing Arts, followed by the Guildhall School of Music and Drama. She graduated in 2010 and went on to book her first television role that same year.

Lily starred in the television series ‘Downton Abbey.’ She played Lady Rose Aldridge and was in 21 episodes from 2012 until 2015. She also portrayed Cinderella in the 2015 remake of the classic movie, and portrayed young Donna Sheridan in ‘Mamma Mia! Here We Go Again.’ In 2020, Lily starred as Mrs. de Winter in the film adaptation of ‘Rebecca.’

Lily started dating Matt Smith in 2014, but their relationship fizzled out by 2019. Although they briefly reconciled in 2020, they split by that summer. In July, Lily was photographed spending time with Chris Evans, but their relationship was never confirmed. In October 2020, she made headlines for looking cozy with her married co-star, Dominic West.
