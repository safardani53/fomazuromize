See Wheel of Fortune star Maggie Sajak's sexiest photos as fans demand Pat's daughter take on bigger

 Posted on June 28, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1090 words  |  Larita Shotwell

PAT Sajak’s daughter Maggie has found her wheelhouse on Instagram.

The blond, bright 28-year-old's risque and witty posts have fans desperate that she takes a Wheel of Fortune hosting role.

Pat, 76, recently announced he will retire in June 2024 after 40 years of hosting the beloved game show.

Ryan Seacrest, 48, will take over for him after the end of next season, marking the end of an era and the start of a new one.

Vanna White, 66, Pat's puzzle board companion since 1982, has lawyered up demanding higher pay - with salary talks reportedly getting "ugly."

But fans are also craving more for Maggie amid the major transition.

Whether it be guest hosting or someday even being the next Vanna, fans reply under nearly all of Maggie's snaps that she has what it takes given her on-set experience, humor, and, yes, the looks.

Currently Wheel of Fortune's social media correspondent and frequently making cameos on-screen - here are Maggie's boldest Instagram photos.

'OH HI!'

In early August, Maggie posed in a tight white top on her page.

Her light blond hair was styled in soft curls, and she donned a low-cut neckline.

She finished off her outfit with a silver choker with the letter M in the center.

"Oh hi!" Maggie wrote under the photo.

One fan replied: "They Should let You be the New Host."

A second comment read: "I still think you should take over for your Dad. I am not impressed that Ryan Seacrest is stepping into your Dad's place on the show. I may just stop watching."

A third wrote: "I was so bummed when you weren't selected to take over for your dad. I certainly hope the show's producers are smart enough to have you take over for Vanna."

SCORCHER IN ORANGE

Earlier this summer, Maggie opted for some wordplay - she smirked at the camera while absolutely glowing in orange.

She captioned an Instagram photo: "Orange you glad it's Friday?!"

One fan commented on her post: "Wish you were going to be the next co-host with Vanna on Wheel of Fortune."

A second fan begged: "Practicing to turn letters? Soon?"

S FOR STUNNING

Maggie went full glam in June, letting her tresses flow across the frame in two snaps.

Maggie's bouncy, voluminous hair cascaded glamorously around her face as if a wind machine blew it into the perfect position - she was seemingly by the sea.

One fan simply commented: "WOW-WEE!!! I hope you take your dad’s place on Wheel of Fortune. I’ll watch it every night!"

Another gushed: "You are very sweet and very gorgeous Maggie."

PRACTICING?

Recently Maggie got in on the Barbenheimer craze by revealing that she went to the summer blockbuster double feature in a snazzy video.

She wore a black sleeveless blazer and a lengthy pleated black skirt- at least, to start.

Behind her was a stage curtain that matched the color of her get-up.

Maggie turned over her shoulder in front of the curtain, and in the blink of an eye, she was sporting a form-fitting pink one-sleeve dress with a thigh slit.

Her gown heavily resembled the style of dresses that Vanna dons on the game show.

Moreover, the quick change was reminiscent of how Vanna reveals letters.

Maggie even mimicked the hands-on-hips pose that Vanna tends to do after she's finished presenting.

She captioned the video: "Me walking straight from Oppenheimer to Barbie... Which new movie matches your vibe??"

Fans picked up the hint as one wrote: "You should be taking over Vanna White’s job when Vanna White is ready to retire."

THE WHEEL SHIRT

Meanwhile, sometimes Maggie celebrates Wheel with her Instagram outfits more literally.

Promoting Teen Week earlier this year, Maggie wore slim bottle green pants paired with a white high-necked cami.

However, it was the cami's design that caught fans' eyes.

Printed onto the top, which showed a little stomach, were mini multi-colored wheels, and devotees quickly wanted one of their own.

One of the show's Instagram followers commented: "So jealous of Maggie Sajak’s top! is it custom?"

Another wrote: "I need Maggie’s shirt!"

PLEATHER & LETTERS

In February, Maggie simmered in skin-hugging pleather pants.

She leaned against Harry Friedman way, the breezeway that connects Wheel and Jeopardy! on the Sony lot.

She pouted for the camera and adjusted her hair, undoubtedly one of the most sultry photos on the passageway between studios.

'I'M STUCK!'

And back in 2022, Maggie donned an extremely short black mini skirt while holding the door of her on-set trailer on the studio lot.

She wore hot pink and captioned the snap: "The sign says to use hand rail at all times so I guess I’m stuck up here now."

Maggie then went on to dutifully advertise a Celebrity Wheel of Fortune episode airing that Sunday.

HAUTE IN HEELS

Earlier last year, Maggie let her long legs do even more of the talking in a stairway shot as she walked around the studio.

She donned high heels and a bold white top with cut-outs along the back.

She wrote: "These heels aren’t made for walking, but apparently that’s what they’ll do!"

ELECTRFYING!

Maggie, in fact, often poses around the Sony lot to promote episodes.

She wrote while in a tiny black dress in front of a batch of generators: "Just generating some excitement for another electrifying episode of #CelebrityWheelofFortune!"

PAT HINTS MAGGIE COULD HOST

Pat's successor Ryan shared a lengthy statement on social media celebrating his role which he'll take on for Season 42.

He wrote: "I'm truly humbled to be stepping into the footsteps of the legendary Pat Sajak.

"I can say, along with the rest of America, that it's been a privilege and pure joy to watch Pat and Vanna on our television screens for an unprecedented 40 years."

Ryan concluded: "I can't wait to continue the tradition of spinning the wheel and working alongside the great Vanna White."

After the news broke, Maggie also tweeted: "It’s so much fun working with my dad…and we’re looking forward to even more fun in Season 41."

That said, Maggie hosting Celebrity Wheel of Fortune or filling in is never off the board.

Her famous dad has given Maggie his seal of approval - she recently brought up her segment hosting Christmas Trivia and said she had a "great time."
