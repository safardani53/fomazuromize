Who is Maren Morris?

Born on April 10, 1990, Maren Morris was raised in Arlington, Texas.

Growing up, she sang in her school's choir and was active in the theatre.

She got her first guitar at age 12 and began writing music shortly thereafter.

Maren performed at various bars and clubs around Texas, with her dad acting as manager and booking agent.

Her debut studio album, Walk On, was released in 2005 when the singer was 15 years old.

Two years later, Maren dropped her sophomore album: All That It Takes.

Maren auditioned for American Idol, America's Got Talent, and The Voice; she was rejected from all three.

After one semester at the University of North Texas, Maren moved to Nashville and was signed to Big Yellow Dog Music as a songwriter.

What is Maren Morris' net worth?

An exact net worth for Maren Morris is not known.

She signed with Columbia records in 2015, and her songs instantly began peaking on various Billboard charts.

In total, Maren has six studio albums:

  • Walk On (2005)
  • All That It Takes (2007)
  • Live Wire (2011)
