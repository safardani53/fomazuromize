Emmy Rossum Goes Topless in Esquire, Brags About Dog's &quot;Big Penis&quot;

 Posted on February 5, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  237 words  |  Martina Birk

Emmy Rossum is not shy.

The Shameless star shared a story about her gynecologist a few months ago.

And now she’s posing with very little clothing on for the new issue of Esquire. To wit:

The brunette bombshell actually conducted her interview with the magazine via Skype, which led to her ruminating about how couples remain close via such technology.

 

“A friend of mine is in a long-distance relationship,” says Rossum. “They have dates on Skype. They’ll both watch the same movie and… play… I assume sex is involved. I don’t know exactly where they stick it in.”

And speaking of private parts, Rossum introduced her dog Cinnamon to the publication and said of the canine: “He has a really big penis.”

Aside from dishing on her pet’s unit, though, Rossum also grew serious and profound.

“It’s amazing how things can be put into perspective for you so easily,” she said. “Last weekend, I was pulling out of my house, and I saw a body in the road and blood everywhere. This guy had been hit by a car. I called 911 and stayed with him…

“You see how lucky you are to just be alive. All the Hollywood bullsh*t and accolades and money really doesn’t matter. It just gives you a slightly nicer house and slightly nicer food and slightly shinier hair.”
