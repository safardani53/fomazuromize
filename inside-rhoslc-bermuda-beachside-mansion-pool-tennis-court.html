Inside RHOSLC stars' $21.5m Bermuda beachside mansion featuring pool, tennis court and outdoor kitch

 Posted on January 1, 0001  |  4 minutes  |  852 words  |  Martina Birk

THE REAL Housewives of Salt Lake City rented out a $21.5 million beachside mansion to stay at for their cast trip, The U.S. Sun can exclusively reveal.

The past two episodes of RHOSLC took place on the Island of Bermuda where the cast ventured to to escape the cold Utah weather.

The U.S. Sun has exclusively learned that the group rented out a luxury property called The Residence in Hamilton Parish.

The beachfront house - that is not on the market - is worth $21,500,000, and is now available to rent as a short-term rental or vacation rental with the price not disclosed unless requested.

There is a minimum five night stay and can range from $12K per night up to $17.5K per night in the peak summer season.

The rate includes a fully dedicated staff with a butler, house manager, concierge, catering, and housekeeping personnel. A private chef is available on request.

It is unknown exactly how much the Housewives paid to stay at the mansion in May 2023 while filming season four.

However, it was made clear that each of the women's bedrooms had a bath tub, except for Meredith Marks.

The drama began day one when Meredith, who was feeling sick and wanted to rest in bed, did not get a bath tub in her room.

Since then, the ladies threw a surprise birthday celebration for newbie Monica Garcia and special pirate-themed dinner that ended with the women arguing inside a cave.

Fighting ensued among all the ladies as Meredith was accused of ruining Angie Katsanevas' reputation, Lisa Barlow continued to slam Meredith for not telling the truth, and Whitney Rose accused Heather Gay of exploiting her sex life.

BEACHSIDE ESCAPE

The massive property has eight bedrooms and 10 bathrooms, which leaves plenty of space for the cast to get ready with their glam team.

It also comes with three powder rooms and the "most dramatic panoramic scenic master bedroom suite."

In addition, there is a professional chef's kitchen, outdoor kitchen, fireplace library lounge, media room featuring table tennis, and a recreational gym.

Outside, there is a tennis court, which is where Angie and Whitney were seen playing tennis one morning, private beach, which is where Heather and Monica were seen taking a morning stroll, and a private pool that comes complete with a hot tub.

Overlooking the South Shore, The Residence is a newly constructed estate resides situated on five-and-a-half acres of land and has become the "largest linear private beach front estate" in Bermuda.

As an added bonus, it is the only private property with a golf cart path directly to a new luxury boutique hotel called The Loren at Pink Beach.

"The Residence was designed for creating an ambiance worthy of private world-class experiences," the real estate listing says.

THE BIG DEBATE

While trying to enjoy the mega mansion - that apparently fits hundreds of Monica's modest home - the cast put the spotlight on Meredith over her alleged DMs sent to children's clothing designer.

An anonymous Instagram DM she received about Angie being a "total fraud" and being in the Greek Mafia became a huge debate about whether Meredith sent it.

The message read: "She is not even close to the level of wealth she represents. In fact one could say she's broke.

"She has current tax lien against her and her husband has declared bankruptcy."

Monica informed the cast that documents were attached to the message that showed Angie owes $170k to the IRS.

The U.S. Sun previously revealed that Angie did, in fact, have a federal tax lien in 2021, but it has since been paid.

According to the Salt Lake County Recorder's Office, the reality star and her husband, Shawn Trujillo, owed the IRS $168,183.00.

They eventually paid off the debt and it was released on December 30, 2021, per court records.

In terms of the alleged bankruptcy filing, there are no bankruptcy filings in either of their names, and it is believed that the person who sent the DM had the wrong Shawn.

Meredith stayed firm and denied that she accused Angie of anything at all. She said: "I have never accused you. I have made no claims of anything. I have not made these claims."

EXPLOSIVE ENDING

The sneak peek for next week's finale shows all comes to blows after Heather finds out some bombshell information.

While on the phone, the Bad Mormon author tells somebody: "I'm freaking the f**k out."

She is then seen pushing cameras away for privacy.

Heather then tells some cast members: "I had some information confirmed today. She's someone that has schemed. I want to confront her. She's not our friend."

Moments later, all the ladies seem to confront one particular cast member over the information Heather received.

Meredith asks: "What did you do?" while Lisa shouts, "Disgusting!"
