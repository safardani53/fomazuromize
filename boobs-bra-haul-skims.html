A FASHION influencer has shared her thoughts on three bras from Kim Kardashian's brand.

The content creator modeled the different Skims looks for her followers.

In her video, TikTok user Julia Pennachio (@juliapennachio) revealed that she wears a cup size 34C.

For her first look, the TikToker posed in the $52 Fits Everybody plunge bra in the color Sand.

"Let me start by saying I feel like I’m wearing a sports bra like I could literally work out in this," she told viewers.

Julia continued: "This bra is absolutely perfect, it’s so thin and light and airy. It does have an underwire, which is great because it gives some support.

"It doesn’t pinch you or hug you [at the back]. It is just a perfect, comfortable, easy-to-wear bra," she said.

Julia also tried on the $52 Fits Everybody T-shirt bra in the color Espresso.

"I feel so confident and sexy in these bras without even feeling like I’m wearing a bra," she said.

Julia added: "This one does have an underwire but it’s not doing any pinching or hugging in the back. This one is great too."

Finally, the influencer shared her thoughts on the $48 wireless form T-shirt demi bra in the color Jasper.

"This is the most comfortable thing I’ve ever put on the girls. It just molds straight on the body, it’s not doing any pinching or hugging in any of the wrong places. I’m obsessed with this," she said.

Julia concluded: "I don’t know how Skims got these bras to fit the body so well but I am obsessed with them and I can’t wait to wear all of them. These will be my new everyday bras."

TikTok users took to the comments section to share their thoughts on the haul.

"OMG, I’m so buying all three!!!" wrote one impressed follower.

Another viewer joked: "I was so close to buying these from this video but I have to remember the boobs don’t come with it."

The official Skims TikTok account described Julia as stunning.
