Kris Jenner critics gasp over her real double chin' as star poses in cloak in pics taken at Balen

 Posted on May 3, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  772 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

KRIS Jenner's critics have gasped over her double chin after the star posed in a black cloak in unedited photos taken at a Balenciaga fashion show in Paris.

The Hulu star's daughter, Kim Kardashian, 43, walked in the show that both she and her granddaughter, North West, 10, attended.

Kris, 68, donned an all-black cloak and bulbous 90s-inspired sunglasses while attending the Balenciaga Haute Couture showcase.

North also sported a 90s look which included platform Crocs and an oversized band tee while at the Paris Fashion Week show.

Even though the event occurred during the summer of 2022, a fan reshared the momager's look to a Kardashian-dedicated Reddit board on Tuesday.

The forum post was titled: "Ah yes, the cult cloak."

"I’m so over the bug-eyed sunglasses they’re trying to make happen," added another.

One commenter asked: "Is that another piece of plastic-chic apparel from the next Khy drop?" before being corrected that the cloak was worn before Khy debuted.

Back in the comment thread, a third person joked: "Bono wants his The Fly shades back."

While another remarked: "Finally showing off her real double chin. Love seeing unedited pics of the [Kar-Jenners]."

KRIS CRITICS

Kim made her catwalk debut during Balenciaga's 51st Couture Collection show as her mother and eldest daughter supported her from the front row.

Fans who caught highlights of the Skims founder's runway walk were quickly distracted by Kris, who grinned ear-to-ear in the audience as her daughter sauntered by.

The then 66-year-old wore a black pantsuit, that was hidden beneath her cloak, and kept on her oversized black sunglasses as she recorded the moment.

Sharing a video of the event to Instagram, Kris wrote: "The most incredible @balenciaga Haute Couture show in Paris today!!

"So proud of you @kimkardashian, and what a runway for the 51st couture collection."

Kim's Paris debut seemed to be overshadowed by her mom's reaction, as fans rushed to an online chat room to compare and dissect her appearance.

"Pretty sure that’s Jack Nicholson," one fan shared, as another countered: "Was going to say the Grinch styled to be in Men In Black but Jack Nicholson is more accurate."

"That’s Kris? I thought it was the Devil from O Brother, Where Art Thou," one person suggested.

And one found Kris' look to be out of this world: "Kris looks like an alien or a grasshopper with those glasses."

SHOCKED FANS

On Tuesday, Kris was captured by Kylie, 26, in a new Instagram photo.

In the post, Kylie snapped pics from the back of a taxi, and wrote: "Mommy nights are the best nights."

The duo seemed to be on the way to a theater in Los Angeles, California on Sunday, where they snuck in and out of the Wonka premiere.

Kris and Kylie were there in support of the latter's boyfriend, Timothee Chalamet, 27, who bagged the starring role in the movie.

In the selfie, Kardashian-Jenner momager Kris wore a silky burgundy jacket, along with a matching shade of dangling earrings.

The Kardashians star pouted at the camera, with her eyes hidden behind shades and her hair in its usual short, tousled style.

On Reddit, however, many fans pointed out the star's unusually drawn face and blamed surgery for her "sunken" appearance.

One fan slammed: "Kris has a**hole lips, lip filler mustache, and sunken cheeks Jesus God."

A second person wrote: "Kris is really looking bad these days. All the surgery is starting to implode."

"I hate the f**king fish lips. Didn't think anyone could look worse than Kylie but her mother looks worse. She too old for this look," blasted a third.

WHO NOSE?

Back in September, Kris shocked fans when she attended Victoria Beckham's fashion show in Paris alongside her partner Corey Gamble, 43, and daughter Kim.

Kris wore a glamorous black dress with a black leather jacket and covered her neck with sparkling jewelry.

However, close-up photos of the star resurfaced on a forum dedicated to the Kardashians, and all attention was on her supposedly collapsed nose.

One person wrote: "Wait. Wtf is up with that nostril?! I'd rather just have a big nose and look old than that. Horrifying."

Another added: "All the money in the world couldn’t help that deflated nostril."
