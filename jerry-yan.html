Jerry Yan Biography

 Posted on April 4, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  240 words  |  Martina Birk

Jerry Yan's career started after he became a model in 2000, after winning the 1998 TVBS Modelling Competition and Men’s UNO modeling competition. He began filming advertisements and starring in music videos. After gaining a success in his modeling career, Jerry began his acting career with his first television series in 2001. Moreover, Jerry worked in the highly popular Taiwanese drama "Metro Garden" and "Metro Garden II" by which he came to the spotlight to the audience.

Jerry was seen in various television series such as "Great Teacher" in 2000 to "Because Love" 2016. Jerry was also seen in the films like "Magic Kitchen", "Ripples of Desire", "Lupin III" and "Our Times". Moreover, is also a known singer, he has released his music albums "Jerry For You", "Freedom", "My Secret Love".

As per the report, Jerry had affairs with four girls in the past. He had an affair with a model-actress Seanna Chang in 2014. After he broke up with her, he was again linked in an affair with Lin Chi-ling, Soko Ikema, and Ruby Lin Xinru simultaneously. In the present condition, Jerry is single and is not interested getting married and having children.

Jerry Yan is 39 years old, Taiwanese nationality actor, singer and a model with Mongolian ethnicity has the height of 5 feet 11 inch. He earns his salary from the film industry and his singing but his exact net worth is still unknown to the media.
