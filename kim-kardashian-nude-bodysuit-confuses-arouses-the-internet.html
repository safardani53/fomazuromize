We’ve seen plenty of Kim Kardashian nude photos over the years.

But we’ve never seen Kim Kardashian nude photos quite like this before. And that’s because Kim Kardashian isn’t actually nude.

No matter what your eyes may tell you upon quick, initial glance.

The reality star is featured in a booklet by System Magazine, posing for a series of sexy snapshots from back when she was rocking platinum blonde hair and walking through the French countryside.

Photographed by Juergen Teller, Kim is seen here in a stain bra over a flesh-colored one-piece, along with knee-high stockings and black boots.

Apparently Kanye West, who also appears in the shoot, selected Kardashian’s unusual outfit.

Kim has been open in the past about the sort of influence her husband has on her fashion.

During an appearance on  Live! With Kelly and Michael earlier this year, Kardashian said Kanye and his stylist once threw away practically all of her clothes, leaving her “crying.”

They left her with "cool, designer stuff," though, that she has come to love.

"My style has evolved and changed. But I couldn’t see it at that time," she added.

It hasn’t evolved and changed all that much, really. We’re still treated to close-ups of Kardashian’s cleavage pretty darn often.
