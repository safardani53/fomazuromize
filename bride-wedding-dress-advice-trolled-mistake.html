PICKING the perfect wedding dress can be pretty stressful, after all there are loads of different options you can go for.

There's classic options, or you could go more glitzy, but this bride decided to rewrite the rules of wedding gowns by picking a very unique dress.

She ordered the dress hoping for the best, but when it arrived the fit wasn't quite right, so she took a few pictures and asked for advice from other brides.

"Please excuse the quality of the picture, I just got my reception dress and it fits perfectly everywhere except my chest area.

Any tips or tricks? Or should I send it back and get the next size up? I'm in serious need of help right now," she wrote.

The dress has a sheer bodice with white hot shorts underneath and a long skirt section with huge splits.

The bra section of the dress didn't fit properly, but people thought that was the least of her worries.

"Yes, send it back. That's it. No need to get a different size," one person commented on the snap.

"It’s somehow a lot and not enough simultaneously," a second added.

"What do you mean it fits everywhere but the chest area? It only EXISTS in the chest area," a third commented.

Meanwhile, others were shocked that she'd want to wear such a revealing dress around her friends and family.

One person said: "Wtf. That is barely a slip. Is she just gonna have her friends and family seeing ALL her no-no bits while she dances?"

"That's something you wear just for your new husband, not your new husband & all your friends and family," someone else said.

"I thought this was meant to go under the actual dress, oh god," another wrote.

It's not clear where the dress is from, but some people thought it might be from bargain sites like Temu or Wish.

"Wish.com, more like Trash.com," someone quipped.

However some people weren't totally against the dress, but agreed it needed a lot of tailoring.

"I see where she's going with it but right now it's a no," someone said.

Another agreed: "It wouldn't be bad if it fit and didn't have the slits."

This isn't the first time a bride's dress has caused a stir on social media though, another woman's dress was cruelly mocked by tolls who said it looked like 'a gingerbread house'.

Her gown is said to be a dupe of a designer one which costs an eyewatering £3,895.

It's not just brides who have got their dress choice wrong - this guest was trolled for her glam outfit as well.

he floor length strapless gown featured floral sequin details, and the guest paired it with matching gloves and even a tiara.

When a picture of her outfit was shared on Reddit people couldn't believe she would dare to wear such a bridal dress to someone else's wedding.
