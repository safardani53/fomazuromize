Is He Dating Harper Murray?

 Posted on February 9, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  562 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Heinrich Haarberg is dating his girlfriend Harper Murray. The couple confirmed it through an Instagram post.

The long list of sensational college quarterbacks keeps growing with Heinrich Haarberg. He is in firey form and looks to reach the top one day.

Moreover, his girlfriend Harper Murray is also an athlete. She plays volleyball for the same university as Heinrich.

Heinrich Haarberg is a 20-year-old student-athlete who plays football for Nebraska Cornhuskers. The young sensation is a quarterback and was born and raised in Kearney, Nebraska.

Furthermore, Heinrich attended Kearney Catholic High School and was a three-star recruit. Then he committed to play for his native state University of Nebraska-Lincoln over multiple other offers. 

Haarberg didn’t play much during his first two seasons, but since 2023, he became the starting quarterback. In addition,  the sophomore is currently leading his team in rushing yards and touchdowns.

Who Is The Girlfriend of Heinrich Haarberg? Meet Harper Murray

Regarding his love life, the young sensation of the Cornhuskers is not single. He is in a relationship with Harper Murray.

Furthermore, Harper is not just another ordinary girl. She is also an athlete and plays volleyball for the Nebraska Cornhuskers.

Harper is 18-year old and celebrates her birthday on February 24. She was born and raised in Ann Arbor, a city in Michigan, to parents Sarah and Vada Murray.

The freshman is currently majoring in business law. But she is in the spotlight of the Cornhuskers Volleyball team and plays the position of an outside hitter.

Harper Has A Wonderful Volleyball Career So Far

Harper Murray is a generational volleyball talent, she was the no.1 overall recruit. In addition, she was also Michigan’s Miss Volleyball 2022.

To add to her list of honors, the student-athlete won Gatorade Player of the Year, and Michigan Volleyball Player of the Year as well.

Furthermore, she was the best spiker in the 2022 U19 Pan-American Cup where Team USA won gold.

Standing at a massive height of 6’2″ the young athlete is the future of women’s volleyball.

Heinrich And Harper: More Into Their Relationship

The two young athletes dating each other is like the most perfect representation of a power couple.

Despite being young, they are the faces of the Nebraska Cornhuskers and are the aces of their respective teams.

So, for Cornhuskers fans, this is a dream come true and the excitement is above the roof.

If we take a deeper look into their beautiful bond, we can presume they started dating recently. It was the volleyball star who broke the news of their relationship with an Instagram post.

She uploaded a picture of the two sweethearts holding hands in the driveway after a Cornhuskers football game on September 26, 2023.

Apart from this, we really have no other forms of cute pictures or typical couple photos of the two love birds.

Heinrich Haarberg: Battling Through All Adversity

For young Heinrich, playing for Nebraska was everything. As his dad, Rod Haarberg was a fullback for the Cornhuskers back in the day, he was eager to follow in his steps.

Moreover, he made it clear by signing the letter of intent as well. But, Heinrich had to wait for his turn and didn’t get any game time for 2 seasons. 

When he did finally get the opportunity, the born-and-bred Nebraska fan let it all loose and showed his true class.
