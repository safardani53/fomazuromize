Camila Angelo started dating Brazilian soccer player Givanildo Vieira De Sousa, aka Hulk, at the age of 34.

Their relationship quickly became the talk of the town, garnering attention for some unexpected twists.

Notably, Hulk had recently gone through a separation from his first wife, Iran Angelo de Souza.

Adding a layer of intrigue to the narrative, it was revealed that Camila Angelo is, in fact, the niece of Hulk’s former wife.

Givanildo Vieira de Sousa, commonly recognized as Hulk, is a Brazilian forward currently showcasing his soccer prowess with Atlético Mineiro.

Having debuted internationally in 2009, Hulk notably contributed to Brazil’s football squad during the 2012 Summer Olympics.

Likewise, his journey with the Brazilian senior team included a triumph in the 2013 FIFA Confederations Cup and a commendable fourth-place finish in the 2014 FIFA World Cup.

FIFA’s official website aptly characterizes him as “a direct powerhouse, whether operating as a center or wide-forward, demonstrating prowess in and around the penalty area.”

Camila Angelo Wikipedia: Age

Born in Brazil in September 1989, Camila Angelo, now 34 years old, holds dual Brazilian-American nationality and has carved out a career as a professional model.

Amassing a considerable following of over 47,000 on Instagram, Camila has transcended her modeling career to become a notable social media influencer.

What adds an intriguing layer to her life story is her familial connection with Hulk’s ex-wife, Iran Angelo de Souza, from whom Hulk separated in July 2019.

Despite the family ties, Hulk and Camila commenced their romantic journey in October 219, while Hulk was still residing in China and playing for Shanghai SIPG.

Their connection, rooted in familial intimacy, culminated in marriage in March 2020, less than a year after they began dating. Their relationship’s swift progression was partly influenced by logistical considerations, including Camila’s need for a visa to continue staying in China with Hulk.

The couple now resides in the United States, owning an apartment in Miami.

Their unique love story, marked by international transitions and family intricacies, continues to capture the attention of those following their journey.

Hulk Expecting Second Child With Second Wife

Former Brazilian striker Hulk has joyfully announced that he and Camila Angelo, his ex-wife’s niece, are expecting their second child.

The couple are already parents to their first child, Zaya, in 2021.

Taking to social media to share the news, Hulk revealed that they initially kept the pregnancy under wraps to inform their children earlier in December.

Expressing concerns about potential leaks, Hulk explained that he wanted to share the news with his children to be a personal one.

Already a father of three from his previous relationship – two boys and a girl – Hueagerly awaits his fifth child’s arrival.

In a heartfelt Instagram story video, he expressed gratitude for the overwhelming messages and shared the excitement of expecting another son, considering it a new blessing from God.

Reflecting on the decision to keep the pregnancy confidential, Hulk remarked,

“I knew they would tell other people and those people would pass it on to someone. I knew it was going to leak.”

It’s worth noting that Hulk was previously married to Iran Angelo de Souza, whom he met in Japan.

The couple tied the knot in 2007 before parting ways in July 2019.

Hulk’s evolving family dynamics continue to capture attention, and this latest announcement adds a new chapter to his journey through fatherhood.
