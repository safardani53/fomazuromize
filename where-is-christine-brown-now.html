Where is Christine Brown now?

 Posted on May 14, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  529 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

CHRISTINE Brown was one of the stars of Sister Wives.

After her departure from the show, she started starring in a Sister Wives spinoff, Cooking with Just Christine.

Who is Christine Brown?

Christine Brown was one of the stars of the Sister Wives reality show that first aired in 2010.

On November 2, 2021, she announced she was leaving her husband Kody Brown after more than 25 years together. 

The couple had "spiritually married" in 1994, with Christine becoming his third wife at the time.

Prior to her departure from the show, she was sister wives with Meri, Janelle, and Robyn.

They were all married to a man named Kody, who was the star of the show.

Where is Christine Brown now?

After Christine departed from Sister Wives, Kody was left with three wives.

On February 20, 2022, TLC shared a preview on its Instagram page about its new digital show, Cooking With Just Christine, which is shown every Sunday on its website.

The caption of the post by TLC stated: "A taste of something new! #SisterWives' Christine will be bringing you some of her favorite recipes on the new digital series #CookingWithJustChristine, dropping every Sunday here and on TLC.com"

During the clip, Christine mentioned that her recipes were delicious and very easy to make.

She continued by saying: "So get your apron ready! Because Cooking With Just Christine is coming to TLC.com."

As the footage played, daughter Truely Grace appeared as she watched her mother's cooking skills. 

Is Christine Brown still married to Kody?

After 25 years of marriage, Sister Wives star Christine confirmed she had split from her husband, Kody in November 2021.

As a result, her departure from Sister Wives makes her the only one to have left the show.

Following her split from Kody, Christine has a new boyfriend, David Woolley.

On February 14, 2023, Christine took to Instagram to introduce the world to David.

In a carousel of photos of the two, Christine captioned the post by saying: "I finally found the love of my life, David. The first time he held me close, it felt like my soul took it's first breath.

Christine continued: "He's wonderful and kind, incredible with my children and an adorable grandpa. I never dreamed I could find a love like this."

Christine and David tied the knot on October 7, 2023 in Moab, Utah.

The former sister wife told People that their wedding was "a fairy tale."

“It's everything I never thought I would have. I get to walk down the aisle to David, him just looking at me the way that he does, it’s a dream come true.”

Per David, Christine finally got her dream wedding, something she never got to do with Kody.

“She wants to be able to wear a white dress and walk down the aisle with her dad,” he shared with the outlet.

“She never got to do on her first marriage. She wants all the bells and whistles.”

How many children does Christine Brown have?

Christine Brown had six children with Kody during their marriage.

They are Aspyn, Mykelti, Paedon, Ysabel, Gwendlyn, and Truely Grace.
